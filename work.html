ReForest Now's rainforest tree nursery can be found in the little-big town of Mullumbimby, Northern NSW. It was established in 2018 with the support of Santos Organics. The nursery is a key part of our work. Growing our own trees ensures we know where the seeds are collected from, how healthy and old they are when planted, and also enables us to engage the local community in our work through volunteer days.
We upgraded our nursery! Thank you to Patagonia, SCCU, Justine Elliot MP (SCR5 Grant), MANA Movement, and Reforest Now donors who funded various aspects of this upgrade.We can now grow upwards of 80,000 trees a year and host many move volunteers in our new space. An exciting development for Reforest Now.
